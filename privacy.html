Privacy Policy — Galico Home Comfort Inc.

Effective Date: November 1, 2025
Company Name: Galico Home Comfort Inc.

1. Introduction

Galico Home Comfort Inc. (“Galico”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you interact with us online or offline, including through our website, social media platforms, lead forms, promotions, and service requests.

By providing your information, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide Directly

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Home address or postal code
  • Details about your home’s HVAC, plumbing, or water heating equipment
  • Responses to forms, surveys, or promotions (including giveaways)

B. Information Automatically Collected

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Pages viewed or actions taken
  • Cookies and tracking technologies (e.g., Facebook Pixel, Google Analytics)

C. Information from Third Parties

  • Facebook Lead Forms
  • Google Ads
  • Referral partners
  • Service platforms used for booking or forms

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and deliver our services
  • Contact you about bookings, quotes, installations, and maintenance
  • Respond to inquiries and customer support requests
  • Send promotional offers, updates, and educational content (with consent)
  • Process entries into promotions or giveaways
  • Verify eligibility for promotions
  • Improve our website, ads, and customer experience
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information with:

  • Service providers (CRM software, email systems, marketing platforms)
  • Technicians and installation crews for service coordination
  • Payment processors (when applicable)
  • Legal authorities if required by law
  • Advertising platforms (Facebook, Google) for ad optimization

All third parties are required to protect your information according to applicable privacy laws.

5. Marketing & Communication Consent

By submitting your information through our website, forms, promotions, or lead ads, you consent to receiving:

  • Phone calls
  • Emails
  • SMS/text messages

You can opt out at any time by:

  • Clicking “unsubscribe” in emails
  • Replying “STOP” to SMS messages
  • Contacting us directly

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Website functionality
  • Analytics
  • Advertising personalization via Facebook Pixel, Google Analytics, etc.

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights (Canada — PIPEDA Compliance)

Under Canadian privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request corrections
  • Withdraw consent
  • Request deletion (where applicable)

To exercise your rights, contact us using the information below.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The revised version will be posted with an updated effective date.

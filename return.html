Return Policy

Return & Refund Policy

Returns Accepted

Yes — returns are accepted under the conditions outlined below.

Return Window

  • Returns must be requested within 30 days from the date of installation or delivery (whichever occurs first).

Condition of Return

To be eligible for a return:

  • The water heater must have been purchased directly from Galico Home Comfort Inc.
  • The unit must have been installed by Galico Home Comfort Inc.
  • The product must be in safe, serviceable condition.
  • The product must not be altered, misused, relocated, or damaged.

Non-Returnable Items

Returns are not accepted for:

  • Products installed by a third party
  • Products that have been modified or tampered with
  • Damage caused by freezing, flooding, electrical issues, gas supply issues, improper venting, or misuse
  • Custom-order or special-order equipment, unless defective

Refund Method

  • Refunds are issued to the original form of payment.

Refund Timing

  • Refunds are processed within 7–10 business days after equipment removal and inspection.

Refund Amount

  • Refunds apply to the equipment purchase price only.
  • Installation, permits, disposal, diagnostic fees, and labor are non-refundable.
  • Any promotional incentives (rebates, gifts, vacations, discounts) may be voided or deducted from the refund amount.

Return Shipping / Removal

  • No shipping is required. Equipment removal is performed by Galico Home Comfort Inc.
  • The customer must provide reasonable access for equipment removal.
  • Removal is scheduled at our earliest reasonable availability.

Exchanges

  • Exchanges are not offered.
  • Defective products are handled under the manufacturer’s warranty.

How to Start a Return

To request a return, contact us within 30 days:

Email: hello@galico.ca
Phone: (343) 464-2219

Please include your name, installation address, invoice number, and reason for return.

Legal Rights

This policy does not affect your rights under applicable Ontario consumer protection laws.

Residential Hot Water Systems You Can Rely On

Hot Water System Installation & Replacement Specialists

Galico is a dedicated hot water system supplier specializing in residential water heater installation, replacement, and upgrades across Ottawa and West Ottawa. Unlike general plumbing companies, our focus is entirely on hot water systems — allowing us to deliver deeper expertise, better product guidance, and safer installations.

We install and replace gas, electric, and tankless hot water systems for homeowners who want reliable performance, predictable operating costs, and code‑compliant workmanship. Whether your existing water heater is aging, leaking, or no longer meeting household demand, we provide clear recommendations based on your home, fuel type, and usage patterns.

Our team handles removal of old equipment, proper venting, gas or electrical connections, and final commissioning to ensure your new system operates efficiently from day one. We also work with leading manufacturers to offer dependable equipment backed by strong warranties. For homeowners looking for long‑term reliability rather than quick fixes, Galico provides a focused, professional approach to hot water system installation and replacement.

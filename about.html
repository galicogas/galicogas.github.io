Service Guarantee

Professional Installation Guarantee

At Galico, every installation is performed to manufacturer specifications, Ontario gas and safety codes, and industry best practices. We stand behind our workmanship and installation standards so homeowners can feel confident that their system is installed safely and correctly from day one.

Transparent Pricing Commitment

We provide clear, upfront pricing before work begins. No hidden installation fees, unexpected add-ons, or last-minute pricing changes — just straightforward recommendations and professional service.

Licensed & Code-Compliant Work

All gas-related work is completed by licensed gasfitters, with proper testing, verification, and commissioning performed before the system is placed into operation. Safety and compliance are never optional steps — they are part of every installation.

Manufacturer Warranty Protection

We install equipment according to manufacturer requirements so your product warranty remains fully valid. When applicable, we assist homeowners with warranty documentation and registration to ensure long-term protection.

Clean & Respectful Jobsite Promise

Your home is treated with care throughout the installation process. Work areas are protected, old equipment is removed properly, and the installation area is left clean and ready for normal use.
